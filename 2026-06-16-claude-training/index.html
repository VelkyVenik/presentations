Part 1 · What is ClaudeVeník

Claude vs Copilot

Different tools for different jobs — not better or worse. Choose your way:
Choose Copilot if…

You live in M365

You need meetings, Teams, and company-wide Microsoft 365 integration baked in.

Choose Claude if…

You need a work assistant

You want a capable AI partner for a wide variety of work tasks, files and apps.

Copilot is the standard solution for everyone. Claude is for when you need to do more advanced things.

Getting Claude

License

You need a license

Claude is licensed — paid through the team budget, not a free-for-all.

Check what you have

My YSoft

See your own and your team's licenses at my.ysoft.com/licenses.

How to get it

Team → budget owner → IT

Agree in your team how you'll use it, confirm with the budget owner, then raise an IT ticket. Please request changes in bulk.

Personal vs Company

Use the company account

Use the company account even for personal things — better that way than the other way around.

Migration

Moving data over?

Not really — there's no clean import from other tools. Start fresh.

Can I have more licenses? Yes — you can add seats and mix Standard / Premium. Coordinate it through the team.
